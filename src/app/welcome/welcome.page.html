Welcome to CineSnips!

How well do you know movie soundtracks?

CineSnips challenges your knowledge by letting you guess iconic scores from your favorite films. Are you ready to test your memory and discover something new?

What is CineSnips?

CineSnips is a fun and interactive trivia game where you guess movie soundtracks across different difficulty levels. Whether your a casual movie fan or a film buff, there's something for everyone! CineSnips is also blind friendly, the app can be fully enjoyed by the visually impared and blind

Compete with friends, track your progress, and see how well you know the music behind the movies.
